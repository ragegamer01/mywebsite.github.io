The Munch box cafe shop

Hello and welcome to the Munch box website. Munch box is a place where you can have a range of meals from breakfasts to sandwiches. Munch box is a cafe where anyone is welcome and you can even take your food away for on the go if you would like. We sell a range of hot food and cold food that are available and we are open Monday to Friday:7AM-2PM and Sat: 8AM-1PM, Sunday closed. There are many meals available and we do breakfast meals and lunch meals and we also do sandwiches and cakes and other foods like sausage rolls. We have special deals every week and also do breakfast and lunch deals. All our products are homemade and freshly baked.
